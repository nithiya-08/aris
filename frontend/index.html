Why SkillBridge?

Skilled workers in rural areas like electricians, plumbers, mechanics lack job visibility and migrate to cities. SkillBridge verifies their skills and connects them to nearby employers.

For Seekers

  • Register with Gmail and Phone
  • Get Skill Passport ID like SKILL-100
  • Upload skills and certificates
  • Take skill test, earn badge
  • Get matched with nearby jobs

For Hirers

  • View verified Seekers
  • Hire locally
